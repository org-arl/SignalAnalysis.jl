Random noise generation

SignalAnalysis.PinkGaussian — Type
struct PinkGaussian{T} <: Distributions.Distribution{Distributions.Multivariate, Distributions.Continuous}

Pink Gaussian noise distribution for random variate generation.

Example:

julia> rand(PinkGaussian(1000))
1000-element Array{Float64,1}:
   ⋮

julia> rand(PinkGaussian(1000, 2.0))
